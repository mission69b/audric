Audric can now generate video.

Describe a scene and it creates a short cinematic clip — no timeline, no editor, just a prompt. It's the newest piece of the creative suite, alongside image generation, editing, and upscaling.

Just ask: *"make a video of a calm ocean wave at sunset."* Pick landscape, square, or vertical (16:9, 1:1, or 9:16) — vertical clips are ready for TikTok and Reels.

![Audric makes video now.](/blog/blog-video-cover.png)

## What you get

- **Free** — one video a day.
- **Pro & Max** — no daily cap, funded by your monthly credit that never expires.

Audric uses **Grok Imagine** — fast, high-quality clips.

![From a prompt to a clip — free and premium tiers.](/blog/blog-video-frame.png)

## Private by default

Like everything in Audric, video runs private by default. Your prompt goes through a zero-retention gateway, and the clip is stored encrypted and private — never on a public URL, never training data.
