Most AI apps ask you to trade your privacy for capability. Audric doesn't.

Audric is a **private, permissionless AI agent** with a non-custodial wallet — built so a normal person can actually own their AI, their data, and their money.

## Private by default

Privacy isn't a setting in Audric — it's how it's built.

- **Zero data retention** — every chat runs through a ZDR gateway. Your prompts and responses are never stored by model providers or used for training.
- **Encrypted, private storage** — your chats and any files you generate are encrypted at rest and never served from a public URL. Only you can read them.
- **Decentralized memory** — optional Private Memory is encrypted on Walrus (decentralized storage, not our servers), recalled only when relevant, and wiped anytime.

## The best models, chosen for you

Open and uncensored models (Kimi, DeepSeek, Grok, GPT-OSS) sit alongside the frontier (Claude, GPT-5.x, Gemini). **Auto** picks the right one for each task — a quick reply on a fast model, deep analysis on a frontier model — so you don't have to think about it.

## A real agent

Audric doesn't just chat. It searches the web live, generates images, and runs visible multi-step research — several live searches shown step by step, then a cited synthesis. Transparent, not a black box.

## Your wallet, your money

Every account comes with a non-custodial Passport wallet, created from your Google sign-in via zkLogin — no seed phrase to manage. Send USDC and USDsui to anyone: free, instant, and gasless. Audric never moves money on its own; every action waits on your tap-to-confirm.
