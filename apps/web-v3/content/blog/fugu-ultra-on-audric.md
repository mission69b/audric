Sakana Fugu Ultra just launched, and it's already in Audric.

Fugu Ultra isn't a single model. It runs on a pool of frontier models and routes each problem to one to three of them, then combines their answers into a single response. On reasoning and scientific benchmarks it lands in the same class as Claude Mythos and Fable 5 — frontier intelligence in one call.

In Audric, it arrives with everything the rest of the lineup has:

- **Private by default** — every prompt runs through a zero-retention gateway. Processed and forgotten, never stored or trained on.
- **A 1M-token context window** — feed it whole codebases, long documents, entire histories.
- **Tools, vision, and reasoning** — it searches the web, runs cited research, reads images, and works through the hard steps.

## How to use it

Pick **Fugu Ultra** in the model switcher, or leave it on **Auto** and Audric routes your hardest problems to it automatically. Like every model in Audric, it shows its real cost up front — no markup games, you always know what you're paying.

Frontier reasoning, private by default, in an AI you actually own.
