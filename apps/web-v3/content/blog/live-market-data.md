Audric brings live market data straight into chat — no plugins, no extra tabs. Ask naturally and Audric picks the right source.

## Crypto

- **Prices & market data** — *"what's the price of SUI right now?"*
- **History** — *"show me ETH's price over the last 30 days."*
- **Screeners** — *"top crypto gainers this week," "trending AI coins."*
- **Market overview** — the global picture plus the Fear & Greed index.
- **On-chain** — new and trending tokens by chain, and research on any token by contract.

## Stocks

- US-equity **quotes, fundamentals, and analyst ratings** — *"research NVDA."*

## Free, auto-routed, private

All of it is **free for everyone** — just ask in plain language and Audric routes to the right skill. Want to see what's available? Browse the [Skills](/skills) page and tap an example to drop it into the composer.

And like everything in Audric, it runs private by default — your queries are never stored or used to train models.
