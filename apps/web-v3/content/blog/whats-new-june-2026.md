We've shipped a lot this week. Here's what's new — all of it private by default, on every model, with your data staying yours.

## Generate and animate video

Audric now makes video. Describe a scene and it creates a short cinematic clip — no timeline, no editor, just a prompt.

- **Free users** get **1 video a day** on our fast model.
- **Pro and Max** unlock the premium video models (Grok Imagine, Veo, Kling) with no daily cap — funded by your monthly credit.

Just ask: *"make a video of a calm ocean wave at sunset."*

## The full image suite — now with upscaling

Image generation and editing were already here. We've added **upscaling**, so the loop is complete: generate an image, edit it in chat, then sharpen it to a higher resolution — all in the same conversation.

Just say *"upscale this"* or *"make it sharper."*

## Live market data, built in

Audric's **Skills** bring live data straight into chat — crypto prices, history, screeners, on-chain trending, token research, and US stocks (quotes, fundamentals, analyst ratings). Ask naturally — *"top crypto gainers this week"* or *"research NVDA"* — and Audric picks the right one. Free for everyone. Browse them on the [Skills](/skills) page.

## Plans that say what you get

We rewrote the plans to be specific. **Free** now clearly includes **20 chats, 10 images, and 1 video a day**, plus web search, research, PDFs, diagrams, the wallet, and decentralized memory. **Pro** ($18/mo) and **Max** ($100/mo) add every premium and frontier model, premium video, and no daily caps — with monthly credit that never expires.

## Still private by default

None of this changes the foundation. Privacy isn't a setting at Audric — it's how the product is built. Every AI request runs through a zero-retention gateway, your data is encrypted, and what can be decentralized lives on storage you control. You'll now see a small **Private** marker in the corner as a reminder: that's always on.
