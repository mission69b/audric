You can now upscale images in Audric.

That completes the creative loop: generate an image, refine it in chat, then sharpen it to a higher resolution — all in the same conversation, with no tools to switch between.

Just say *"upscale this"* or *"make it sharper."*

## One conversation, the whole image suite

- **Generate** — describe what you want.
- **Edit** — *"add a glow," "remove the background," "make it warmer."*
- **Upscale** — sharper, higher resolution, ready to use.

It works on images you generated and on photos you upload.

## Private by default

Your images stay yours — stored encrypted and private, never on a public URL, never training data.
